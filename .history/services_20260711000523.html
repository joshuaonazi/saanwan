What We Do

Seven disciplines,
one production partner.

From the first planning call to the final master, SAANWAN Consulting covers the full multimedia pipeline so you're not stitching together five different vendors.

01Project Management

Project Management

Expert project management to ensure the successful execution of multimedia projects — from initial planning and resource allocation to timeline management and quality control. Our project managers oversee every stage to deliver results that meet, and exceed, client expectations.

02Media Training

Media Training

Comprehensive media training programs that build essential skills in media relations, public speaking, interview technique, and crisis management — so you can confidently navigate any media platform and engage your audience with clarity.

03Sound Design

Sound Design

Sound design for film, television, gaming, and advertising. Our sound designers use modern tools and creative expertise to build immersive soundscapes that enhance storytelling and elevate the overall audio-visual experience.

04Videography

Videography

Professional videography for corporate videos, commercials, music videos, documentaries, and more. Our videographers capture compelling visuals that resonate with audiences and carry your message with impact and authenticity.

05Music Production

Music Production & Orchestration

A full music production and orchestration studio, where composers and musicians collaborate to create original compositions tailored to your project — from orchestral arrangements to contemporary tracks for film, television, and advertising.

06Audio Engineering

Audio Engineering

Recording, mixing, mastering, and post-production sound editing from experienced audio engineers, equipped with modern gear and software to ensure clean audio and seamless integration with your visuals.

07Entertainment

Entertainment

Talent management, event production, and artist development. We support artists and entertainers in reaching their creative and professional goals from live performances to digital content creation.
